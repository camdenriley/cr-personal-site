That's what I bring to every session. Not just knowledge of the game. The game itself."

The philosophy

Coached by a pro.
Built for the next one.

I founded CR Academy because I know what it takes to make it — and I know how much the right coaching at the right age can change everything. I was that kid once, putting in extra work because I wanted it more than anything.

Every session I run is built around real game situations. Not drills for the sake of drills. Not cone courses that look good on video. Situations that replicate what actually happens on the pitch — because that's what makes players better.

🎯

Game-realistic training

Every drill, every sequence, every rep is designed to simulate real match pressure and decision-making.

🧠

Technical + mental development

Skills are only half of it. We develop game IQ, composure under pressure, and the mentality to compete.

📈

Individual progression

Every player gets a training plan built around their specific needs, position, and goals.

"Most people don't end up where they thought they would — and that's not a bad thing. Life changes, priorities shift, and what once felt right starts to feel a little different. The goal isn't to have it all figured out. It's just to keep moving towards what feels right for you. So maybe the question isn't, why change? Maybe it's — what feels right next?"
— Camden Riley
